How to Play

WordMatch Arena · ESL Championship

🎲

Roll for First Turn

Both players roll a die. Higher roll goes first. Ties re-roll automatically.

🃏

Flip Two Cards

On your turn, flip any two cards. Try to match an English word with its meaning.

✅

Match = Extra Turn

Found a match? You keep your turn and flip again. Miss? Cards flip back and it's the other player's turn.

⚡

Combos

Match pairs quickly (within 5 seconds of each other) to earn combo bonuses on top of your base points.

🔥

Difficulty Levels

Rookie = 8 pairs · Warrior = 12 pairs · Legend = 18 pairs.

🏆

Win Condition

All pairs matched — highest score wins. Rematch or go back to settings from the winner screen.

⚡ Scoring System

Match = +100 pts
Combo x2 = +150 pts
Combo x3 = +200 pts
Miss = Turn Switch
