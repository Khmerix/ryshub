Grammar Focus: Tag Questions

Forming and using tag questions to confirm information or express attitudes

A Forming Tag Questions (10 points)

📌 Grammar Rule:

Tag questions are short questions added to the end of statements.

  • Positive statement → Negative tag: You're funny, aren't you?
  • Negative statement → Positive tag: He doesn't like comedy, does he?
  • Use the same auxiliary verb (be, do, have) or modal (can, will, etc.) as the main sentence.
Add the correct tag question to each statement.

2. She has a great sense of humor, ?
3. They didn't understand the joke, ?
4. He can make anyone laugh, ?
5. You haven't seen this movie before, ?
